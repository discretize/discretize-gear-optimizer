The gear optimizer is currently in beta! Templates are not final and illusion/summon/mech and lifesteal and condition-on-crit damage is inaccurate. Please report potential issues to us in Discord Github.

Select your traits, then select and customize the trait bonuses you want to simulate using the checkboxes below each line. Many bonuses are conditional and may not apply to your setup.Only the bonuses with checkboxes are applied here, so be sure to change the Skill Coefficients section when changing to/from traits without checkboxes like Lingering Curse or Legendary Lore for accurate results.
